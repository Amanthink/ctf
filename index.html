Interactive Math Notes

Limits & Continuity

Definition: A limit describes the behavior of a function as it approaches a specific point.

Example: \( \lim_{x \to 2} (3x + 1) = 7 \)

Try finding: \( \lim_{x \to 5} (2x^2 - 3) \)
Derivatives

Definition: The derivative measures the rate of change of a function.

Example: \( f(x) = x^2 \), then \( f'(x) = 2x \).

Find \( \frac{d}{dx} (x^3 + 2x) \).
Multivariable Calculus

Definition: Functions with more than one variable (e.g., \( f(x, y) \)).

Example: \( f(x, y) = x^2 + y^2 \) is a function of two variables.

Find \( \frac{\partial}{\partial x} (x^2y + 3y) \).
